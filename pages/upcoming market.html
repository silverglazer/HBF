Mission Statement

At Hanging Barrel Farms, we are dedicated to providing the highest quality produce and products to our community. Our mission is to cultivate sustainable farming practices that respect the environment while delivering fresh, organic goods directly from our farm to your table. We believe in the power of local agriculture to nourish our bodies and strengthen our community ties.
